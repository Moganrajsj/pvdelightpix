Pre‑Wedding Shoot in Dharmapuri

Home / Pre Wedding

Capture your story together before the wedding rush begins.

A pre‑wedding shoot is the perfect time to slow down, laugh together and create photos that feel like your relationship. PV Delight Pix designs relaxed, cinematic pre‑wedding sessions in Dharmapuri and nearby locations tailored to your comfort and personality.

Why choose PV Delight Pix for your pre‑wedding shoot?

Guided yet natural posing: Even camera‑shy couples look comfortable with simple prompts and easy directions.

Location ideas around Dharmapuri: From lakesides and temple streets to green fields, the team suggests locations that match your vibe.

Wardrobe & styling tips: Help with coordinating outfits, colours and accessories so everything looks cohesive on camera.

Cinematic compositions: Creative framing, depth and light that make your photos look like stills from a film while still feeling real.

Our pre‑wedding photography services include:

  • Candid Pre‑Wedding Photography: Unscripted moments, laughter and inside jokes captured as you spend time together.
  • Themed Pre‑Wedding Shoots: Styled concepts – traditional, casual, vintage or minimal – planned to suit your personalities.
  • Destination & Outdoor Sessions: Shoots at scenic locations in and around Dharmapuri or at your chosen destination.
  • Indoor / Studio Pre‑Wedding: Clean, timeless portraits in studio lighting for couples who prefer privacy.
  • Save‑the‑Date & Album Ready Images: High‑resolution files ideal for invitations, social media and wedding decor.

What you can expect from a PV Delight Pix pre‑wedding shoot

  • Pre‑shoot discussion on locations, outfits and overall mood (fun, romantic, minimal, editorial).
  • Session plan with timing around the best light – usually early morning or golden hour evenings.
  • Professionally edited images with colour grading that matches your wedding photography style.
  • Option to combine pre‑wedding with wedding day photography packages for better value.
