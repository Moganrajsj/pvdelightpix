Birthday Photoshoot in Dharmapuri

Home / Birthday Photoshoot

Make Every Birthday in Dharmapuri a Story to Remember

From cosy first‑birthday cake smashes to colourful kids parties and family milestones, PV Delight Pix creates birthday photos that feel joyful and real. Shoots can be done at our studio in Dharmapuri or at your home / event venue, depending on what suits your little one best.

Why choose PV Delight Pix for your birthday photoshoot?

Kid‑friendly team: Shoots are paced with patience so kids have space to warm up, play and be themselves – no stiff, forced poses.

Colourful yet timeless edits: We retain the fun of balloons, cakes and décor while keeping skin tones natural and flattering.

Studio or outdoor options: Choose between our controlled studio setups, your decorated home, or parks and open spaces around Dharmapuri.

Complete coverage: From candid moments during cake cutting to group photos with family and friends, we capture the full story of the day.

Our birthday photoshoot services include:

  • Kids Birthday Sessions: Portraits and candid moments of your child enjoying their special day.
  • Cake Smash Shoots: Fun, messy and adorable first‑birthday sessions with simple props and backdrops.
  • Milestone Birthdays: Elegant photoshoots for 16th, 18th, 21st and other special milestones.
  • Family & Sibling Portraits: Group photos that include parents, siblings and grandparents.
  • Event Coverage: On‑location photography for birthdays hosted at halls, farms or resorts in and around Dharmapuri.

What you can expect from a PV Delight Pix birthday shoot

  • Pre‑shoot call to discuss theme, outfits, cake smash ideas and timing that suits your child’s routine.
  • Guided yet natural posing so kids feel encouraged, not pressured.
  • Professionally edited images delivered as soft copies, with options for prints and albums.
  • Flexible packages to cover just portraits, just event coverage or a mix of both.

Celebrate your child’s big day with photos you will love revisiting for years. Share your date, venue and preferred style, and PV Delight Pix will suggest birthday packages that fit your plans and budget.
