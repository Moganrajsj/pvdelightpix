Wedding Photography in Dharmapuri

Cinematic, heartfelt wedding photography

PV Delight Pix documents your wedding with a balance of cinematic flair and honest emotion. From intimate home rituals to grand receptions, the focus is always on real connections between you, your families and your friends.

Why choose PV Delight Pix for your wedding?

Story‑first approach: Every event – from mehendi and sangeet to muhurtham – is photographed as part of one continuous story, not isolated poses.

Candid + classic blend: Expect a mix of natural, candid frames and a few guided portraits that parents will love framing.

Experienced team: The crew is familiar with South‑Indian and North‑Indian wedding rituals, ensuring important moments are never missed.

Colour‑true edits: Wedding photos are edited with balanced tones that keep your outfits, décor and skin tones looking natural and timeless.

Our wedding photography services include:

  • Candid Wedding Photography: Unposed moments of laughter, tears, hugs and inside jokes throughout the celebrations.
  • Traditional Coverage: Clean, well‑lit photos of rituals, stage events and family groups for albums and prints.
  • Pre‑Wedding & Engagement Shoots: Couple sessions at favourite locations in and around Dharmapuri.
  • Haldi, Mehendi & Sangeet: Vibrant coverage of all pre‑wedding functions.
  • Wedding Albums & Films: Custom‑designed albums and highlight films that weave the full story together.

What you can expect when you book PV Delight Pix

  • Pre‑wedding consultation to understand your rituals, venues and expectations.
  • Clear timelines, shot priorities and coordination with your planner or family members.
  • Backup workflows to keep your images safe from the memory card to final delivery.
  • On‑time delivery of curated, fully edited photographs and optional albums.
