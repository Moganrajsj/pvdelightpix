About Us

Home / About Us

Crafting stories from Dharmapuri to destinations across South India.

PV Delight Pix is a boutique photography studio based in Dharmapuri, Tamil Nadu, led by photographer Jagadish and a small, dedicated team of visual storytellers. The studio is known for its cinematic wedding films, emotive candid images, and calm, friendly presence on the wedding day.

What began as a passion project has grown into a full–time studio trusted by couples and families across Dharmapuri, Salem, Krishnagiri, Bangalore and beyond. The team specialises in documenting real emotions – the stolen glances, inside jokes, and quiet in–between moments that often go unseen.

From intimate home ceremonies and baby showers to multi–day weddings and corporate events, PV Delight Pix delivers carefully edited images and films that feel timeless but never over–processed. Every assignment is treated like a one‑off story, with lighting, framing and pacing tailored to the people in front of the camera.

Clients often describe their experience with PV Delight Pix as “patient, adaptable and enthusiastic”. The team gently guides couples when needed, but also knows when to step back and let moments unfold naturally. This balance keeps everyone relaxed in front of the camera and results in photographs that feel honest and unforced.

MEET THE FOUNDER

Jagadish – Photographer & Creative Lead

Jagadish started PV Delight Pix with a simple idea: every couple deserves photographs that feel like them. Over the years he has developed a style that blends clean composition, natural light and a touch of cinematic drama. He believes in building a connection with each family so that the camera never feels intimidating.

When not shooting weddings, Jagadish is often exploring new locations around Dharmapuri for pre‑wedding sessions, experimenting with lighting setups, or refining the studio’s editing workflow so that clients receive their memories faster without compromising on quality.
